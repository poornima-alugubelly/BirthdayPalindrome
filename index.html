Birthday Palindrome
Enter your birthdate and we will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome
This app checks your birthdate in 6 formats yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yyyy, dd-mm-yy, mm-dd-yy , yy-mm-dd e.g. if your birthdate is 01 Aug 1995, then app will check for 19950801, 01081995, 08011995, 010895 , 080195,950801
